      Caveats & known limitations

        Data-driven, not deterministic. The Reddit dataset is heavily self-selected (~93–99% of reports are acceptances), so percentiles are relative to admitted students, not raw applicant pools. Treat every verdict as a floor estimate, not an admission probability.
